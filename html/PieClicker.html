Pie Clicker is a cookie clicker knock off where you just click the pie to increase your score. You can buy upgrades with your pies in order to make it easier to get a higher score. Once you reach 200000 pies, you can prestige, losing all your upgrades, pies, the more prestige points you have, the more pies for click you get to start with. I plan to add different minigames as well, allowing you to gamble your pies to get more pies
